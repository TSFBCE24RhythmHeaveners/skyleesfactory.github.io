Fonts

Here are a few small fonts I've made in a web app called fontstruct. It's not a good way to make fonts in my opinion, but it is fun to play around with. If you want you can look at and download (some of) the fonts from my fontstruct page!

These two are fonts I've used for coding, and both are proportional alterations of Jetbrains Mono. The left one is based on maximal letter differentiation, in that every letter is as unique as possible, which is why it's so quirky and wacky.

Lastly, this one is what I currently use to code, with Jetbrains Mono on the right. They're identical, except for the i, l, w, W and m, which have all been adjusted to not be monospaced. It makes it read nicer to me, and the overall density of text isn't thrown off by the otherwise very tight lowercase m.
