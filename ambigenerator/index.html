Ambigram generator

This is my ambigram generator, or ambigenerator for barely short. You can either type a word in the box and click generate, and the generator will make a bunch of rotationally symmetric ambigrams of that word. If you type two words separated by a space, those will be merged into a single ambigram with the two words being the two readings.
