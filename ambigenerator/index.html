Ambigram generator

This is my ambigram generator, or ambigenerator for barely short. You can type a word in the box and click generate, and the generator will make a bunch of rotationally symmetric ambigrams of that word. If you type two words separated by a space, those will be merged into a single ambigram with the two words being the two readings. Choose below one of the three styles of ambigrams available, and click the ambigrams to rotate them.

The point is that it makes way too many ambigrams, so that one of them is hopefully legible enough that can show it off to your friends.

Created by me in pure HTML, CSS, and a bit of JS. Theme is Dracula. Thank you for visiting!!
